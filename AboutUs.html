About Us

Nepal Angels Network is made to assist early-stage startups and business owners in making connections with knowledgeable angel investors who are looking to fund exciting new ventures. Nepal Angels Network is the place to be if you're an entrepreneur searching for finance or an investor looking for the next big thing.

Learn More

Who are we?

Nepal Angel Network is a not for profit organization established on 21st May 2022. The organization strives to connect the investors in Nepal keen to invest in aspiring businesses. The main type of investing would be seed investment in ideas and in startups. The network members have numerous investment avenues and entrepreneurial investment propositions being only one of them. Therefore the value proposition of the venture and the investment opportunity need to be compelling. Please do look at the suggested funding application as it gives the minimum details for a business plan to be considered.

Our Mission

Our mission is to identify and support exceptional entrepreneurs with innovative ideas, providing them with the necessary capital, expertise, and resources to achieve their goals.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create an ecosystem where innovative ideas and entrepreneurship can thrive, helping to drive economic growth and improve people's lives.

Our Goal

  • Identify and invest in promising early-stage companies with high growth potential and a strong vision for the future.
  • Provide support and resources to portfolio companies, including mentorship, networking opportunities, and access to industry expertise.
  • Foster a collaborative community of investors and entrepreneurs, promoting knowledge-sharing and cross-collaboration.
